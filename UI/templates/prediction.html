Welcome to AI Medical Scanner

SkinGuard AI is a smart skin-image classification model designed exclusively for dermatology. It analyzes uploaded skin photos to detect abnormalities and provides instant, reliable predictions. The model identifies whether a lesion is benign or malignant and supports both single-image uploads and ZIP file inputs, delivering fast and accurate results every time.

ThoraxAI is an AI-powered deep learning system that analyzes chest X-ray images and predicts the disease present in the uploaded scan. The user uploads a single X-ray, and ThoraxAI processes it, identifies abnormalities, and gives the most likely disease along with a confidence score. It also recommends the right type of doctor based on the predicted condition, making diagnosis faster, easier, and more accessible.
